Weekly Challenges

Challenge 1

Warm UP

  • Practice deep breathing exercises for 10 minutes daily.
  • Engage in a physical activity you enjoy for at least 30 minutes each day.
  • Write down three things you are grateful for every night before bed.
Challenge 2

Bonding

  • Reach out to a close friend or family member and share how you're feeling.
  • Create a positive affirmations list and read it aloud every morning.
  • Practice mindfulness meditation for 15 minutes daily.
Challenge 3

Lets Feel Better

  • Avoid negative self-talk and replace it with positive affirmations.
  • Engage in a hobby or activity that brings you joy for at least 1 hour each day.
  • Practice relaxation techniques, such as progressive muscle relaxation or guided imagery.
Challenge 4

Bring Posetivity

  • Create a daily self-care routine and stick to it.
  • Engage in activities that promote relaxation, such as taking a bath or practicing yoga.
  • Challenge negative thoughts by reframing them with positive and realistic alternatives.
