Facts About Ludo

  • Ludo is a popular board game that originated in India.
  • The game is played on a square board with four colored pieces per player.
  • Each player has to race their pieces from the starting point to the center of the board.
  • Ludo is a game that brings people together and encourages friendly competition.
  • It is enjoyed by people of all ages and is a great way to spend quality time with loved ones.
