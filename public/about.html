About ZenZone

Welcome to ZenZone, a platform designed to provide support, resources, and tools to help individuals overcome depression and improve their mental well-being.

Our goal is to create a safe and inclusive community where individuals can find solace, share their experiences, and access valuable resources that promote mental health.

Through a combination of diagnostic assessments, daily tasks, weekly challenges, and engaging games, we aim to empower users on their journey towards emotional wellness and self-discovery.
