Facts About Sudoku

  • Sudoku is a logic-based number placement puzzle.
  • The objective is to fill a 9x9 grid with digits so that each column, each row, and each of the nine 3x3 subgrids contains all of the digits from 1 to 9.
  • Sudoku puzzles became popular internationally in the late 20th century.
  • The modern Sudoku was most likely designed by American puzzle maker Howard Garns in 1979.
  • It gained significant popularity in Japan in the 1980s before spreading worldwide.
