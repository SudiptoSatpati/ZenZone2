Facts About Chess

  • Chess is a two-player strategy board game played on a checkered gameboard.
  • The game is believed to have originated in India around the 6th century.
  • Chess is played by millions of people worldwide and is considered a sport and an art.
  • It is a game of infinite possibilities, with each game presenting unique challenges and opportunities for strategic thinking.
  • Chess is widely recognized for its ability to improve critical thinking, concentration, problem-solving skills, and creativity.
